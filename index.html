ABOUT ME

I make video games and I animate as well.

Welcome to my website. I make 2D Artwork primarily in charcoal as well as colored pencil. Illustration has always been a passion of mine. In the future, I hope to utilize these techniques to create 2D and 3D games as well as animated content and other forms of multimedia.

Saif Chowdhury


I graduated from Texas Tech University with a Bachelors in Computer Science and pursuing a minor in art. My primary focus is digital painting for the purpose of creating video game assets. I have years of experience developing Indie 2D games utilizing knowledge from both my art skills as well as my coding knowledge. Here are some of my latest art works.
